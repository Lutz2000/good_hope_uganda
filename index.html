About Our Charity

Giving hope.

Good Hope Foundation Kids Uganda is an NGO nonprofit that I started up to reach out to the homeless, Orphans and street children to meet their basics needs of life. We focus on providing God’s word, food, shelter, clean water, clothes, education and health services. I am the founder of the community (MUBIRU HENRY, Kampala Uganda, Tel: +256 708 257 217 Bwende Village) As a servant of GOD, I developed the idea since I knew how it felt to be a street ghetto kid, sleeping under the coldness of trenches, sleeping on empty stomachs and at times collecting empty bottles of water around Kisenyi in Kampala. The back grounds of the kids are untold as the so soft touching as I start narrating on how I collected each of them. I am looking forward to molding up these little ones to be important citizens with God’s guidance in our country. Being neglected since childhood does not end the world, Everyone has his own ending on the Earth. Join me and help us to make a difference by putting a smile on these kids of mine with provision of necessities.
